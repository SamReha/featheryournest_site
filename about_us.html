about us

about us

Feather Your Nest founder and principal Gina Carroll has a keen eye for what works and what doesn't work in any room of a home. Since 2007 she and her team have staged homes throughout the Bay Area resulting in higher selling prices and fewer days on the market. Helping realtors and sellers meet maximize each listing is her primary goal.

Prior to Feather Your Nest, Gina had a successful 20+ year career in advertising and marketing communications, specializing in technology-based businesses. Today she uses the skills and expertise honed in her advertising career to expertly prepare a home for market.

She’s backed by a creative and hard-working crew who have a combined total of more than 25 years of staging experience.
