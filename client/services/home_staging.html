occupied home staging

This service is designed for sellers who will continue to live in the home while it is on the market.

We will do a complete walk through with your Realtor and create a detailed improvement list from the front gate to the back yard and everything in between.

During the consultation, we will help determine what items remain, and what improvements will need to take place before our team arrives to work its magic and bring out the best in your home.

We will supply supplemental furniture, area rugs, lighting, art, and accessories to best showcase the home.

Fee: $250 for walkthrough Staging Consultation. Staging cost varies with size of home, number of rooms to be staged, and overall scope of work.
