about us

Since 2007, Gina Carroll has built Feather Your Nest Staging, one of Silicon Valley's premium home staging firms. She is an excellent communicator and a seasoned marketer who understands the needs of both buyers and sellers. Her mission is to team with realtors in explaining the benefits of staging to sellers, offering detailed pre-staging recommendations and specific information to improve the perceived value of any listing.

Prior to Feather Your Nest Staging, Gina had a long, successful career in technology advertising and marketing communications. A proud mom of three grown sons, Gina lives in a renovated 1960 California ranch style home in San Jose. She designed and supervised every lovely detail.

Gina has a BA degree in Philosophy and a Master of Education (M.Ed.).
