Feather Your Nest Staging

Feather Your Nest supplies a complete range of real estate staging services, from staging consultations, occupied home stagings, and vacant home stagings. We provide all the furniture, art, accessories, lighting, linens, and other essential items to turn your listing into a showcase property.

We offer the following staging services:

Vacant Home - Our expert team will evaluate your property, then design and install furniture, art, lighting, and accessories to fit the style of the home. We have a warehouse full of elegant furnishings to complement the property, even including the small but important details like the right shower curtain and welcome mat.

Occupied Home - This is designed for sellers who will continue to live in the home while it is on the market. We can help evaluate what to keep and what to pre-pack and make recommendations on paint, carpeting, and other finishes if updating is needed. We are able to edit what you have and bring in staging inventory as needed to complete the look.

Staging Consultation - Before going on the market, a Staging Consultation provides valuable feedback on how to best showcase the home’s potential. This is critical information for the seller and the realtor. The consultation includes a walk-through of the home and a detailed list of recommendations to showcase the home’s assets.

Benefits of Staging

Staged homes sell faster and for higher prices. The cost of staging can be recovered many times over in the final sales price. Staging fees are part of the cost of selling a home, and may be tax-deductible. Consult your tax advisor. Call to schedule a consultation.
