Vacant Home Staging

Vacant homes look cold and impersonal. Create a welcoming and stylish environment for buyers with staging. It will show the home to its fullest potential and help buyers visualize living in this home.

Feather Your Nest will provide furniture, art, lighting, and accessories to fit the ambiance and style of the home.

Each staging is unique. Our designers create a well-coordinated and contemporary look designed to appeal to today’s buyers. Our team leaves the staged home completely photo ready.

Costs vary by home. Call for more information.

Benefits of Staging

Staged homes sell faster and for higher prices. The cost of staging can be recovered many times over in the final sales price. Staging fees are part of the cost of selling a home, and may be tax-deductible. Consult your tax advisor. Call to schedule a consultation.
